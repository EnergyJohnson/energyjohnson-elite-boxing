The Story

Born in the Ring, Built for Excellence

My journey in boxing began in the heartland of British fighting, the storied gyms of London, where raw talent meets relentless discipline. I was fortunate to learn under the guidance of legendary trainer Don Charles, the man behind Derek Chisora's rise to world title contention. From him, I absorbed the art and science of elite-level boxing from the very best.

"Boxing isn't just about throwing punches. It's about discipline, confidence, and becoming the strongest version of yourself, inside and outside the ring."

What followed was a career that spanned continents. I became a fixture in London's professional boxing scene, training alongside two-time world heavyweight champion Anthony Joshua and heavyweight contender Derek Chisora. I coached Frank Buglioni to British and European championship glory. I trained with ten-time BJJ World Champion Roger Gracie in Brazil and the UK, adding grappling mastery to my striking expertise.

From Italy to Spain, Ireland to Africa, Brazil to the Middle East, I have shared my knowledge across more than fifteen countries, training professional fighters, elite athletes, and high-profile private clients who demand nothing less than the best.
